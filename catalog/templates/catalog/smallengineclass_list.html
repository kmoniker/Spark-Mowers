Small Engine Repair Classes

Learn how to make money fixing lawn mowers

My name is Casey Christensen. I have years of experience tuning up lawnmowers and running my own lawnmower tune up /repair business. I have also taught many classes including small engine repair classes for several years.

About the Class

It will be taught in 2 three-hour sessions (or one long day if we have to). Dates will be determined when we have interested parties. We are looking for mature teens who can sit through and stay engaged in the class.

This class will teach students all about small engines. We'll concentrate mainly on the systems operating within a lawnmower - taught all with hands-on work with actual machines. Students will understand how small engines work by taking apart an engine and putting it back together. They will also learn how to tune up lawnmowers by properly installing spark plugs, checking and cleaning air filters, changing the oil, cleaning out the carburetor. Simple repairs such as repairing pull cables and springs will be taught as well.

I'll also give practical business advice on how to run your own lawnmower repair/tune up business. You can make enough to earn your tuition money back and more in one day! Learn how when you take the class. Some students who took my class in the past have started their own businesses!

Tuition: $125 per student.
