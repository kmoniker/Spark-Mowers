Why get a tune-up?

One Pull Start

Lawn mower getting hard to start? Most lawn mowers are one tune-up away from starting on the first pull!

Better Lawn Health

A dull lawn mower blade is like trying to cut grass with a baseball bat. A freshly sharpened blade gives cleaner cuts and a healthier lawn.

Improve Fuel Efficiency

An annual lawn mower tune-up can improve your fuel effeciency by up to 30%. A tune-up also extends the life of your lawn mower and reduced costly repairs.

What is included in a tune-up?

Change the oil

When was the last time your oil was changed? Most manufacturers recommend an annual oil change to keep your lawn mower runing smoothly for years to come.

Sharpen the blade

A dull blade will rip your grass instead making a clean, healthy cut. It'll also cut faster, meaning less time spent mowing the lawn.

Replace the spark plug

A fresh spark plug can improve fuel effeciency and make your lawn mower easier to start.

Clean the air filter

A dirty air filter in your lawn mower can reduce fuel effeciency by more than 30 percent and can make your lawn mower difficult to start up.

Clean the chassis

Dirt and grass that builds up on the engine and deck can prevent proper air flow and cooling. We'll clean it out for you.

Fast Service

We'll come pick up your lawn mower and service it, then return it to you. Usually done in a couple of days.
